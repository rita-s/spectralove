Communication Style Matching

Why communication-style matching changes everything.

Tinder matches on photos. Hinge matches on prompts. SPECTRA matches on how you actually communicate — 7 communication style pairs profiled during onboarding, then matched for complementarity, not similarity.

Proactive ↔ Reactive

Who initiates, who reflects

Proactive: "Let's go!" Reactive: "Let me think about it." Matched for balance — one drives, one steers. The engine coaches each style differently.

Toward ↔ Away-From

Motivation direction

Toward: "What could we gain?" Away-From: "What should we avoid?" The engine adapts prompts — motivating vs. reassuring language, matched to each person.

Procedures ↔ Options

Structure vs. exploration

Procedures: step-by-step. Options: open-ended. Your Options + their Procedures = creative vision + structured execution. The matching engine sees this.

General ↔ Specific

Big picture vs. detail

General thinkers get broad questions. Specific thinkers get concrete ones. Matched pairs get prompts calibrated to both — "big idea first, then zoom in."

Similarities ↔ Differences

What you notice first

Similarity seekers: "We both love that!" Difference seekers: "That's so different from how I see it!" Both valid — the engine frames shared topics through each lens.

Internal ↔ External Authority

Where confidence comes from

Internal: "I know what I think." External: "What do others say?" The coaching adapts — "What's your take?" vs. "Here's what the research shows."

Conversation Coaching

6 coaching tools working invisibly in every conversation.

The engine analyses your chat in real-time and delivers subtle, optional suggestions. Both users know coaching is active. It teaches better conversation, not manipulation.

Language

Language Matching

Detects communication preferences — visual, auditory, or action-oriented language — and suggests vocabulary that resonates with your match.

Balance

Conversation Balance

Detects who's talking more and gently rebalances. "Share your own experience too" — prevents monologues and one-sided dynamics.

Timing

Emotional Timing

Suggests emotionally engaging questions at peak moments. Creates positive associations tied to the conversation — at the right time, not forced.

Perspective

Perspective Shifts

"Try asking how they see it" — shifts perspective when conversation gets one-dimensional. Builds empathy naturally without being preachy.

Depth

Depth Regulation

"Zoom in — ask for a specific example." Shifts conversation from abstract to concrete when energy drops, or zooms out when it gets too granular.

Flow

Flow Detection

When conversation is flowing deep and natural, the engine goes silent. Best coaching is knowing when not to coach. You'll never see a prompt during flow state.
